## Reciprocity
  - More likely to say yes to those that they "owe"
  - Be the First to Give- Make sure that what you give is personalized and unexpected
  - **Ex:** Christmas- someone brings you a gift, have to give a gift back
## Scarcity
  - People want more of things that there are less of
  - What is unique?
  - What do they stand to lose?
## Authority
  - More likely to listen to people in positions of authority
## Consistently
  - Looking for commitment
  - Initial commitment can lead to future
## Liking
  - 3 factors:
  - Similar to us
  - Compliment
  - Cooperate towards mutual goals
## Consenus
  - Looking at the actions of others in order to determine their own
